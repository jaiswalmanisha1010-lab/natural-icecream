About Natural Icecreame

Welcome to Natural Icecreame, one of the most loved ice cream shops in Kandivali East, Mumbai. Our mission is simple — to serve delicious, fresh, and natural ice cream that makes every customer smile.

Located near Thakur College Kandivali East, our shop is a favorite place for students, families, and ice cream lovers. We use quality ingredients and natural flavors to create the perfect ice cream experience.

At Natural Icecreame, we believe ice cream is not just a dessert — it is a moment of happiness. Every scoop is made with love, freshness, and the best taste.
